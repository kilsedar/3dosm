Class: GeoJSONParserOSM

GeoJSONParserOSM(dataSource)

Extrudes GeoJSONGeometryPolygons and GeoJSONGeometryMultiPolygons, and uses ShapeAttributes extended by "extrude", "altitude", "altitudeMode" and "heatmap" in the OSMBuildingLayer.
